Prototype
◎

Ideal customer for this dashboard

An owner-operator who's also the COO of a B2B services firm that runs on renewable contracts and a lean team — juggling cash, clients, and pipeline with no finance or RevOps function. They’re buried in tabs and spreadsheets and don’t need another dashboard — they need the handful of things that actually need them today surfaced, drafted, and ready to approve, so the business runs without them living inside it.

●Staffing & recruiting — placements, CSM book, renewals (modeled here)
●Managed IT / MSPs — recurring contracts & account health
●Marketing & creative agencies — retainers & pipeline
●Professional services — bookkeeping, HR, fractional firms
Team size
5–50 people
Revenue model
Renewable contracts
Back office
Owner-run, no RevOps
⊟

What it replaces

My honest read: this doesn't rip out your accounting system — it kills the tab sprawl and the spreadsheet layer an owner stitches together to actually run the business. One place, and an agent that does the work instead of just showing it.
